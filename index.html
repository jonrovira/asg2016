Our Vision

ASG is a student government. We’ve lost sight of what our responsibilities entail when looking at this. I wish for asg to be the

We believe there needs to be a paradigm shift in what ASG is to this campus--how we engage with our students and student groups. I want for each of my peers to believe in us as a resource--as someone who can answer their question or guide them in the right direction. I want for each of my peers to believe in what ASG can do.

Because to me, that’s the beauty of student government. The opportunity of what asg can do is something that we need to better take advantage of--to better seize. Currently, we are maximizing the utility this organization could provide to our campus; this is for a number of reasons.
